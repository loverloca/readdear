Why did we make readdear!

Addressing the Detrimental Impact on Young Minds

The prevalent scroll-down method, widely adopted across the Internet, is eroding children's attention spans, reducing their focus to less than one minute.

Challenges in Child-Friendly Platforms

Today, a disturbing number of children's platforms inadvertently expose them to adult content, thereby jeopardizing their safety and well-being.

Promoting the Right to Read

Our mission is to provide children with a platform where they can read without interruptions. We believe that reading should be accessible and free, allowing children to engage with valuable content without the need for endless scrolling on social media.
