why did we make readdear!

BRAIN DESTROING


The scroll-down method, which has become popular all over the Internet, disrupts the attention span and reduces it to less than one minute.

BAD CHILDREN'S PLATFORMS

The majority of children's platforms now contain adult contents. as a result; The majority of them become harmful for our kids.

THERE IS NO READING, AND IT IS NOT FREE.

The kids don't have the choice to read as he has to scroll down on social media, and that is because reading is not free and accessible.

ReadDear aimed to:

cfree a free and easily accessible platform

for children, making it convenient for them to immerse themselves in the joy of reading

Create a platform for children

Ensures their safety from any form of abuse that could negatively impact their childhood. The platform provides a secure environment where parents can confidently leave their children, knowing that it serves as a beneficial shelter for them.

Creating a platform that enhances a child's concentration rate

through reading is an effective solution to the problem because reading is a process that demands a significant amount of concentration and time.
